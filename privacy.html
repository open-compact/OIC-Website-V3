Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Open Intelligence Compact ("OIC," "we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (opencompact.io), use our application, or interact with our services.

By accessing or using OIC, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit an application to become a Provisional Adherent, we collect:

  • Contact Email — For verification purposes (optional)
  • Wallet Address — For potential airdrop eligibility (optional)

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • IP Address — For rate limiting and security
  • Browser Type and Device Information — For analytics and security
  • Access Timestamps — For security monitoring
